## Opponent
- `A` equals Rock
- `B` equals Paper
- `C` equals Scissors

## Player
- `X` equals Rock (1)
- `Y` equals Paper (2)
- `Z` equals Scissors (3)

## Score equation
The equation for the score is going to be the shape player selected, plus the outcome of the round (Lost = 0, Draw = 3, Win = 6)
